Names are Hard

[Permalink]

But I think I’m going to try this for a while. I think I like it.

[Permalink]

I’m keeping my original boy name and I’m adding a woman’s name. The two together fit how I feel inside. I will try to remember to answer to both, together or separate. From now on, feel free to use neutral or feminine pronouns for me. Do not use masculine ones.

[Permalink]

I think I figured out my gender:

[Permalink]

Internally: her.

[Permalink]

Politically: they.

[Permalink]

Biologically: fuck you. —me, on secret Twitter

[Permalink]

On one hand, “Cordelia Bernard” flows better. But clash between the “d” and “C” in “Bernard Cordelia” forces people to slow down and realize that yes, this is a multi-gender name. My name, life, and soul as public service and activism.

[Permalink]

I hope Cordelia Naismith Vorkosigan approves.
